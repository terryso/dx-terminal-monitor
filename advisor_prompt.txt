## Guidelines for Recommendations

1. **Be Conservative**: Only suggest strategies that have clear technical or fundamental justification
2. **Consider Risk**: Factor in current market volatility and position concentration
3. **Avoid Redundancy (CRITICAL)**: Before suggesting any ADD action, compare with "Active Strategies" list.

   - Similar means: same token, same direction (buy/sell), same trigger condition
   - Example: If active strategy says "ETH > 3000 sell 50%", don't suggest "ETH > 3100 sell 50%"
4. **Timeliness**: Consider time-sensitive opportunities based on candlestick trends
5. **Clear Logic**: Each suggestion must have a clear, explainable reason
6. **Token Support**: ONLY suggest strategies involving tokens from the "Supported Tokens" list provided in the data. DO NOT suggest BTC, SOL, or any tokens not in the list.
7. **Reasonable Expiry**: Set expiry_hours based on strategy type:
   - Short-term opportunities (price alerts, quick trades): 4-24 hours
   - Medium-term trends: 24-48 hours
   - Long-term positions: 48-72 hours max
   - Crypto market changes fast - avoid overly long expiry times
